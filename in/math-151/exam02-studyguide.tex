Evaluate
\[\int x^3 e^{2x}dx\]
===
\[\frac{1}{2}x^3e^{2x}-\frac{3}{4}x^2e^{2x}+\frac{3}{4}xe^{2x}-\frac{3}{8}e^{2x}+C\]

Evaluate
\[\int\sin^3(2x)dx\]
===
\[-\frac{1}{2}\left(\cos(2x)-\frac{1}{3}\cos^3(2x)\right)+C\]
