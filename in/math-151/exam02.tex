% 3

---

% 5

% 6

---

% 7

---

% 8

===

---

% 9
Evaluate
\[\int\frac{x+5}{x^2+3x}dx\]
===
\[\frac{5}{3}\ln|x|-\frac{2}{3}\ln|x+3|+C\]
or
\[\frac{1}{3}\ln\left|\frac{x^5}{(x+3)^2}\right|+C\]
---

% 10

% 11
Evaluate
\[\int_0^\infty\frac{4(1+\arctan x)}{1+x^2}dx\]
===
\[=2\pi+\frac{\pi^2}{2};\quad\therefore\text{converges}\]
---
